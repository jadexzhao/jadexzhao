# values

**clarity**
say what you mean. build what you say. don't make people guess.

**care**
the person on the other end of the system is a real person. design like it.

**integrity**
do the work even when no one is watching. especially then.

**breadth**
curiosity is not a liability. the intersection is where the interesting things live.

**service**
build things that matter for people who need them. not just things that are impressive.

**patience**
big seasons take time. bloom anyway.
