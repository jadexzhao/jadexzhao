# client discovery questionnaire

*used at the start of every matcha green consulting engagement.*

---

**about the organization**
- what does your organization do, and who do you serve?
- what problem are we solving together?
- who is the primary audience for this project?

**about the project**
- what does success look like for this project?
- what has been tried before, and what happened?
- what are the hard constraints? (budget, timeline, technology)

**about the relationship**
- who is the main point of contact?
- how do you prefer to communicate and how often?
- what does a good working relationship look like to you?
