# year by year

*a living document. updated as seasons close.*

---

**year one ... 2023-2024**
arrived at bloomington as a hudson and holland scholar not entirely sure what informatics meant in practice. left the year knowing. serve it changed how i think about building things. the w in russian culture was the first time i let something go. that mattered too.

**year two ... 2024-2025**
heavier and better. interaction design research. hci. math foundations. the middle way house redesign. accessibility audit for the city. started to feel like someone who actually builds things, not just studies them. made the dean's list both semesters.

**year three ... 2025-2026**
the f in info infrastructure ii over summer was a hard lesson. fall was full but good. then madrid. madrid was its own year inside the year. came back different in ways i'm still finding words for.
